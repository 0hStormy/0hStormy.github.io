Some music for Gingerbug - 3/1/25

Hey! It's been a hot minute since I've updated my website. But I'm back with some music for a game I'm working on!

Me and my friend Ren have been working on a game together. Ren has been doing most of the programming and art while I'm doing the music and some other things.

Here's a little summary of what the game is about:

Explore and survive the Gingerbug Isles, their depths, their seas and their skies. Build a fort alone or with friends, fight the beasts across the land, and defend your home from coordinated attacks with a variety of weapons, vehicles and war machines, all while sustaining yourself and your technology.
